HISTORY OF JAME

My name is Andrea Medeghini. I began working on JAME in 2000, when I was a student at University. I continued to rework my software year after year until 2008 when I completed JAME 6. After this release I planed to continue adding new features, but since 2009 I have been very busy with other projects and I couldn't delivery new releases after 2010. The source code hasn't been maintained for long time since then, but last September 2014 I began reworking my software. I decided to deliver the last maintenance release of JAME in 2015 and then continue working on a fresh new project which will soon replace JAME.

The beauty of caos

JAME is an application for creating amazing fractals. It supports continuous zoom of Mandelbrot and Julia sets. You can explore fractals and record animations. You can compose multiple layers and you can apply filters and effects to your images.

JAME is a free software project hosted by SourceForge and GitHub.

Requirements

JAME requires Java 7 or later and a computer with a multi-core CPU and 1Gb of RAM. JAME is available for Mac, Windows and Linux.
